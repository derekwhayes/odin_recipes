Microwave Mexican Rice

The Recipe

This no-fuss microwave Mexican rice is quick and simple. Using instant rice, it comes together in a flash, and
goes great with tacos, burritos, and enchiladas.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups instant rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup picante sauce
  • 1 (4oz) can chopped green chiles
  • 1/2 (1oz) envelope taco seasoning

Steps

  1. Place minute rice, water, picante sauce, green chiles, and taco seasoning in a large microwave-safe baking
    dish. Stir until evenly combined.
  2. Microwave, uncovered, on High for 8 minutes. Let sit for 5 minutes to absorb liquid. Fluff with a fork and serve.
